🎮 Explore the EnterX World

Dive into our living, breathing voxel universe! Use WASD to traverse landscapes, mouse to survey your domain, and clicks to reshape reality itself.

🎮 Controls

WASD Move around
Mouse Look around
Left Click Break blocks
Right Click Place blocks
1-5 Select block type

🧱 Block Palette

Position: 0, 0, 0
Selected: Grass
FPS: 60

📖 How to Play

🚶 Use WASD keys to move around the world
👀 Move your mouse to look around
⛏️ Left-click to break blocks and collect them
🧱 Right-click to place the selected block type
🎨 Use number keys 1-5 or click buttons to select block types
